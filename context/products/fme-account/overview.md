# FME Account

> Product context for **FME Account**. Edit sections as your understanding evolves.

## Product Overview

FME Account (also called Safe Account) is the unified identity platform for Safe Software's entire product ecosystem. A single account provides access to FME Flow Hosted, FME Community, FME Academy, FME Hub, and Support — with more services planned. It supports profile management, password management, email updates, and two-factor authentication (2FA/MFA). The account portal lives at account.safe.com. FME Organizations — currently in development — will extend FME Account to support enterprise self-administration of licensing, users, and product settings.

**Core Value Proposition**: FME Account (also called Safe Account) is the identity platform underpinning the entire FME ecosystem — it is the single source of truth for customer identity, authentication, and account management across all Safe products.

**Tagline**: *One identity for the entire FME Platform.*

## Strategy / Positioning

FME Account is the identity foundation that ties the FME Platform together, reducing friction across Safe's growing product surface and enabling a seamless single sign-on experience for customers. As Safe expands its product portfolio and moves toward subscription-based licensing, a reliable, centralized identity layer becomes increasingly critical — both for customer experience and for Safe's ability to manage entitlements, enforce security, and build organizational controls. FME Organizations (in development) represents the next evolution: turning FME Account from a personal identity tool into an enterprise administration hub.

## Unique Approach

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Single identity across the platform** | One account unlocks FME Flow Hosted, FME Community, FME Academy, FME Hub, Support, and future services — no separate credentials per product |
| **Security-first design** | Two-factor authentication (2FA/MFA), email confirmation for changes, and account recovery workflows built in |
| **Enterprise extensibility** | FME Organizations (in development) will add multi-user administration, licensing control, observability, and settings management for subscription customers |
| **Platform growth enabler** | As Safe adds products and subscription tiers, FME Account serves as the identity and entitlement layer that new services can plug into |

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Unified login
Single sign-on across FME Flow Hosted, FME Community, FME Academy, FME Hub, and Support via account.safe.com.

### 2. Profile management
Username, avatar, personal information, and email address management in one place.

### 3. Two-factor authentication
Optional 2FA/MFA via authenticator app for enhanced account security.

### 4. Password & account recovery
Self-serve password reset via email; account recovery support via accountrecovery@safe.com.

### 5. FME Organizations *(in development)*
Self-service administration of FME deployments for subscription customers, covering licensing, user management, observability, and product settings.
