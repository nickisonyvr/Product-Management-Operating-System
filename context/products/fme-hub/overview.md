# FME Hub

> Product context for **FME Hub**. Edit sections as your understanding evolves.

## Product Overview

FME Hub is a free community platform for discovering, sharing, and downloading customized FME content — including transformers, formats, templates, and web connections. Content is published by three tiers: Safe's own development team (official), external vendors (verified), and the broader FME community. Users can download freely, and with an account can publish their own content privately or publicly, bookmark items, and receive update notifications.

**Core Value Proposition**: FME Hub is Safe's marketplace where Safe and the FME community can publish, discover, and share custom content — transformers, packages, web services, and more — extending the FME Platform for every use case.

**Tagline**: *Extend FME. Share with the community.*

## Strategy / Positioning

FME Hub extends the value of the FME Platform by enabling a community-driven ecosystem of reusable content, reducing the time customers spend building common integrations from scratch. By lowering the barrier to contribution and discovery, it strengthens platform stickiness and positions Safe as the center of gravity for the data integration community. A tiered content model (Official → Verified → Community) gives customers confidence in quality while keeping the platform open to innovation.

## Unique Approach

| Pillar | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Tiered content quality** | Three content tiers — Official (Safe), Verified (vendors), and Community — so users can calibrate trust and quality when selecting content |
| **Free & open access** | All content is freely downloadable; an account unlocks publishing, bookmarking, and update notifications |
| **Community-driven extension** | Any FME user can publish transformers, formats, templates, and web connections, extending the platform beyond what Safe ships out of the box |
| **Integrated discovery** | Content is discoverable and installable directly within FME Form and FME Flow, reducing friction between finding and using new capabilities |

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Content discovery
Browse and search transformers, formats, templates, and web connections published by Safe, vendors, and the community.

### 2. One-click download & install
Access and install content directly from the Hub into FME Form and FME Flow.

### 3. Publishing tools
Create and upload custom content, with documentation for item preparation, workspace testing, and publication workflows.

### 4. Private & public sharing
Authors can store content privately or share it publicly with the broader FME community.

### 5. Bookmarks & notifications
Save favourite items and receive update notifications when content is revised or new items are added.

### 6. Monthly digest
Subscribe to a digest of newly published community content.
